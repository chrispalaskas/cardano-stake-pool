What is Cardano?

Cardano is a blockchain platform for changemakers, innovators, and visionaries, with the tools and technologies required to create possibility for the many, as well as the few, and bring about positive global change. Cardano is a proof-of-stake blockchain platform: the first to be founded on peer-reviewed research and developed through evidence-based methods. It combines pioneering technologies to provide unparalleled security and sustainability to decentralized applications, systems, and societies.

What is Staking?

Cardano uses Ouroboros as its proof-of-stake protocol. It distributes network control across stake pools: node operators with the infrastructure required to ensure a consistent and reliable connection to the network. For each slot, a stake pool is assigned as the slot leader, and is rewarded for adding a block to the chain. Ada holders may delegate their stake to a specific stake pool, increasing its chance of being selected as the slot leader, and share in the stake pool’s rewards.

Why Prefer Skepsis Pool?

We are people with technical backgrounds and have the firm belief that technology can and should improve our lives. We are strong believers of the success and mass acceptance of cryptocurrency and we support the Cardano network especially because of its great community, underlying scientific foundation and vision. What better way to propagate these ideas than through young, smart college students? Hence, we donate part of our profits to support at least one student every year.

We are also environmental sensitive and despise the huge amounts of energy consumed by the proof of work protocol to maintain blockchains such as Bitcoin. It was a necessary evil to bring the cryptocurrency world here, but we must evolve. We found Cardano's Proof of Stake protocol Ouroboros appealing. Not only we do not have racks of ASIC miners running but we host our 3 pool nodes on Virtual Machines on Microsoft's Azure Cloud, which has been CO2 neutral since 2012. On top of that we have 0% tax on our delegator's rewards, maximizing their profits.
